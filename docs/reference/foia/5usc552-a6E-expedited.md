# 5 USC 552(a)(6)(E) — Expedited processing

Agencies must provide for **expedited processing** of requests when the
requester demonstrates a **compelling need**, or in other cases the agency
determines by regulation. A compelling need exists where:

1. failure to obtain the records on an expedited basis could reasonably be
   expected to pose an **imminent threat to the life or physical safety** of an
   individual; or
2. the requester is **primarily engaged in disseminating information** and there
   is an **urgency to inform the public** about actual or alleged Federal
   Government activity.

The agency must determine whether to grant expedited processing **within 10
days** of the request and must process expedited requests **as soon as
practicable**. A requester asserting a compelling need must certify the
statement is true and correct to the best of their knowledge and belief.
