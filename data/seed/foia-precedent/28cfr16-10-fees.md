# 28 CFR 16.10 — DOJ fees

Components charge fees in accordance with this section and the OMB fee
guidelines unless a waiver or reduction applies. Fees reflect the requester
category determination (commercial / educational-scientific / news-media /
other) made under 5 USC 552(a)(4)(A).

Components do **not charge** fees if the cost of routine collection and
processing of the fee is likely to equal or exceed the amount of the fee.
Components ordinarily collect all applicable fees before sending copies of
records, and may require **advance payment** where the fee is likely to exceed
\$250 or where a requester has previously failed to pay a fee in a timely
fashion.
