# 5 USC 552(a)(6)(A) — Time limits

Upon any request for records, an agency must determine within **20 working
days** (excluding Saturdays, Sundays, and legal public holidays) after receipt
of the request whether to comply, and must immediately notify the requester of
that determination, the reasons for it, and the right to appeal any adverse
determination to the head of the agency.

In **unusual circumstances**, the time limit may be extended by written notice
to the requester for a period of **up to 10 additional working days**, setting
out the reasons for the extension and the date on which a determination is
expected. Unusual circumstances include the need to search separate field
facilities, to examine a voluminous amount of separate and distinct records, or
to consult with another agency having a substantial interest in the records.
