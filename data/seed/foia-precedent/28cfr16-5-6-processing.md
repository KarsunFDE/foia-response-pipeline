# 28 CFR 16.5–16.6 — DOJ processing of and responses to FOIA requests

**§ 16.5 (Timing of responses).** Components ordinarily respond to requests
according to their order of receipt. Multitrack processing may place simple
requests in a faster track than complex ones. Where the statutory time limit
cannot be met because of unusual circumstances, the component notifies the
requester in writing and offers an opportunity to limit the scope or arrange an
alternative time frame.

**§ 16.6 (Responses to requests).** The component notifies the requester of its
determination, any fee charged, and the right to seek assistance from the
component's FOIA Public Liaison. An **adverse determination** — a denial in
whole or part, a "no records" response, or a denial of a fee waiver or expedited
processing — must inform the requester of the **right to appeal** to the Office
of Information Policy and the right to seek dispute-resolution services from the
Office of Government Information Services (OGIS).
