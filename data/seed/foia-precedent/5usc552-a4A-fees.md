# 5 USC 552(a)(4)(A) — Fees and fee categories

Agencies set fees limited to reasonable standard charges for document search,
duplication, and review. The chargeable fees depend on the **requester
category**:

- **Commercial use** requesters may be charged for **search, review, and
  duplication**.
- **Educational / non-commercial scientific institutions** and
  **representatives of the news media** may be charged for **duplication only**,
  with the first 100 pages provided free.
- **All other** requesters may be charged for **search and duplication**, with
  the first 2 hours of search time and the first 100 pages provided free.

Fees may be **waived or reduced** if disclosure is in the public interest
because it is likely to contribute significantly to public understanding of
government operations and is not primarily in the commercial interest of the
requester.
